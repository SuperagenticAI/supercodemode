# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and users

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
